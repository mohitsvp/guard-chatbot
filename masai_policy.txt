# **Masai School Guardrails Policy**

## **1. Purpose**

This policy defines the expectations, responsibilities, and acceptable behaviors for the Masai School community — including students, instructors, staff, and mentors — to ensure a positive, inclusive, respectful, outcome-driven learning environment aligned with Masai’s mission and values. ([Masai School][1])

---

## **2. Core Principles**

Masai is built on the following foundational principles:

1. **Outcome-Driven Learning:** Everything we do focuses on real, measurable student success. ([Masai School][1])
2. **Respect & Professionalism:** Members treat each other with courtesy, respect, and dignity.
3. **Ownership & Growth Mindset:** Individuals take responsibility for their learning, performance, and conduct.
4. **Integrity & Accountability:** Honest work, ethical behavior, and full academic effort are non-negotiable.
5. **Collaboration & Community:** Supporting peers and contributing to a constructive learning culture.

---

## **3. Code of Conduct**

### **3.1 Academic Integrity**

Students and staff must:

* Submit original work and properly credit sources.
* Not engage in plagiarism, cheating, or sharing/submitting others’ work as their own.
  **Violation Consequence:** Immediate review and potential termination from the program. ([Masai School][2])

### **3.2 Respectful Communication**

Masai community members should:

* Communicate professionally in all interactions (classes, Slack, Zoom, communities).
* Avoid rude, disrespectful, discriminatory, or harassing language.

### **3.3 Collaboration & Participation**

* Engage actively in scheduled sessions, assignments, and group activities.
* Ask questions when in doubt — proactive engagement is encouraged. ([Masai School][2])
* Support peers, foster inclusive dialogue, and build a strong cohort community.

### **3.4 Attendance & Commitment**

* Attend all mandatory sessions, submit assignments on time, and follow the curriculum schedule.
* Avoid chronic tardiness, absenteeism, or disregard for deadlines.

### **3.5 Responsible Use of Tools/Platforms**

* Use learning platforms and communication channels (e.g., Slack, Zoom) appropriately and respectfully.
* Do not misuse or exploit technical or platform privileges.

---

## **4. Data & Privacy Expectations**

Masai collects and protects personal data in compliance with applicable laws (e.g., Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023). Participants should:

* Provide accurate information at registration.
* Consent to communication for administrative and service-related purposes.
* Respect confidentiality of shared information. ([Masai School][3])

---

## **5. Behaviour Around Academic Work**

### **5.1 What to Do**

* Solve your own problems; seek help from instructors/mentors when stuck. ([Masai School][2])
* Collaborate ethically — discussions are good, copying is not.

### **5.2 What Not to Do**

* Don’t procrastinate or skip assignments. ([Masai School][2])
* Don’t be a passive learner.
* Do not isolate yourself — build connections for mutual growth. ([Masai School][2])

---

## **6. Professional Conduct in Placement Support**

During career support and hiring activities:

* Present authentic work and profiles to employers.
* Maintain professional behavior in interviews and employer interactions.

---

## **7. Grievance Redressal**

Masai provides designated channels for concerns about conduct, academic issues, or policy violations. Participants may submit grievances in writing and expect acknowledgment plus resolution efforts as per institutional protocols. ([Masai School][4])

---

## **8. Enforcement & Consequences**

Failure to adhere to this policy may result in:

**For Students:**

* Warnings (verbal or written)
* Academic probation
* Suspension or termination from access
* Ineligibility for completion certificates or placement assistance (in severe violations related to conduct or payments) ([Masai School][4])

**For Staff/Mentors:**

* Formal review
* Corrective action
* Termination of affiliation

Masai reserves the right to enforce this policy in line with legal standards and internal procedures.

---

## **9. Amendments**

Masai may update this policy over time to reflect evolving standards and organizational needs. Changes will be communicated through official channels and become effective as notified.

---

## **10. Acknowledgment**

All members (students, staff, mentors) must read, understand, and acknowledge this policy as a part of onboarding and yearly reaffirmation.
